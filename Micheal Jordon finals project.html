Who is Micheal Jordon

Michael Jeffrey Jordan, also known by his initials MJ, is an American businessman and former professional basketball player. His profile on the official National Basketball Association website states that "by acclamation, Michael Jordan is the greatest basketball player of all time. Jordon won six championships with the Chicago Bulls was named Finals MVP all six times. He was the NBA Most Valuable Player five times and a 14-time All-Star. He was All-Star game MVP three times and Defensive Player of the Year. He was all-defensive first team nine times and the NBA scoring leader 10 times.
